Proceeding of the 3rd International Conference on Science and Technology

Held as part of the Universitas Gadjah Mada Annual Conferences (UASC) 2017 series, the 3rd UGM International Conference on Science and Technology (ICST 2017) provides an ideal academic platform for researchers to present the latest research findings and describe emerging technologies and directions in engineering and the natural sciences. This year, five symposia will be held, the 5th ANNUM Conference, the 2nd Computer symposium, the 2nd Geomaritime symposium, the 1st OMICS symposium: from Genomics to Metabolomics, and the 1st Infrastructure Technology symposium. The selected papers will be published in journals that are indexed by Scopus or DOAJ/EBSCO: the Indonesian Journal of Chemistry, Indonesian Journal of Geography, Indonesian Journal of Biotechnology, IEEE Xplore digital library, the Proceedings of Scientific.Net (Applied Mechanics and Materials; Advanced Engineering Forum).

Date of Conference: 11-12 July 2017
Conference Location: Yogyakarta, Indonesia
Editor in Chief: Prof. Coba Coba Dulu
Managing Editor: Siapa Yang Mau ; Dialah Namanya
Editorial Board: Nama Pertama ; Nama Selanjutnya ; Ada Lagi Nggak
